About Me

I'm Razin Agida Kaharuba. Some people also refer to me as Agid. I am a passionate game developer and software engineer. I like to make games and cool things that make the little difference that counts in the world. Other than that, I like to make some dumb but suprisingly useful apps for myself sometimes.

I studied Software Engineering at the University of Technology Sydney (UTS). But I have a lot of experience when it comes to making games. I'm born Indonesian but have lived in various countries like Malaysia and Australia.

I love playing video games as a hobby, it means a lot to me. I play almost any genre of games, some of my favorites are strategy, colony sim and RTS. I play a bunch of Minecraft, Dota 2, Age of Empries and Satisfactory.

I also have some other hobbies like cooking and badminton. I have recently picked up the guitar again. I also like to follow topics around space, technology and science.
